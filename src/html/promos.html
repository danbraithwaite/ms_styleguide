Promos

Promotional messages are occasionaly used on the site, whether it’s to let the user know that there’s a sale on a certain furniture range, cheaper shipping or a big promo/ad for an M&S app (see food promo below). In most cases, you shouldn’t be using a lot of promo blocks on the same page. They work a little differently to alert messages, thus different rules apply.

Small text promo

In this example, we have a promo for an offer/sale of a product. So, we use the M&S promo/offer red colour. As this is a small promo, it’s purpose is purely to inform - but it should not be shouty or overpowering.

Save 20% when you buy 2 or more pieces of selected furniture
  • Font weight: light
  • Font size: 18px
  • Colour: #c4201d
  • Box border: #c4201d
  • Box fill/bg: #ffffff

Large full width promo

In this example, we have a promo for the M&S cooking app. As the promo needs to have links to download the app, a heading, body copy and image of the app, it needs to be fairly large.

Free app full of delicious recipes

Try our Cook With M&S recipe app. It's packed with inspiration and clever features, such as built-in timers and personalised shopping lists. It's your helping hand in the kitchen.
